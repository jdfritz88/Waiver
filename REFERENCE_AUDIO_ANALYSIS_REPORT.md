## 5. RECOMMENDATIONS FOR WAIVER APP

### A. Pitch Range Settings

**Current Settings:**
```python

'glissando_end_semitones': 8
```

**Recommended Settings (based on analysis):**
```python
'glissando_start_semitones': -13  # Allows lower starting pitch
'glissando_end_semitones': 19      # Allows higher peak pitch
```

**Rationale:**
- Reference audio spans -13.1 to +21.5 semitones (buildups) and -12.9 to +19.5 semitones (orgasms)
- Setting to -13/+19 covers most of the emotional range
- This gives 32 semitones of range (vs previous 16 semitones)

### B. Breathing Frequency

**Current Setting:**
```python
'breathing_frequency': 15  # 15% chance per clip
```

**Recommended Setting:**
```python
'breathing_frequency': 3  # 3% chance per clip
```

**Rationale:**
- Reference audio has breathing ~3.2% of the time
- Breathing occurs roughly every 31.6 seconds
- With 4-second clips, 3% = ~1 breath every 33 seconds (matches reference)
- Previous 15% setting would be ~1 breath every 6-7 seconds (too frequent)

### C. Energy/Volume Settings

**Current Behavior:** Volume increases during orgasm (1.15x multiplier)

**Recommended Change:** **REDUCE or REMOVE orgasm volume boost**

**Rationale:**
- Reference orgasms are 0.25x the energy of buildups (much quieter!)
- Current app behavior contradicts reference audio pattern
- Consider: Keep volume same or slightly reduce during orgasm
- Add breathiness increase instead of volume increase

**Suggested Implementation:**

## 8. TECHNICAL DETAILS

### Analysis Methodology:

**Pitch Tracking:**
- Algorithm: YIN (superior for vocal tracking vs piptrack)
- Frequency Range: 80-800 Hz (vocal range)
- Voiced Frame Filtering: f0 > 100 Hz (removes unvoiced/noise)

**Energy Analysis:**
- RMS energy calculation with 512-sample hop
- Frame-level energy profiles
- Peak and average energy metrics

**Vocalization Event Detection:**
- Onset strength envelope calculation
- Onset detection for individual vocalizations
- Inter-vocalization interval analysis

**Breathiness Estimation:**
- Zero Crossing Rate (ZCR) as proxy
- Higher ZCR = more airy/breathy quality
- Range: 0.0462-0.0600 in reference

**Tempo Analysis:**
- Beat tracking on onset envelope
- BPM range: 106-141 BPM
- Correlates with emotional intensity

- 10 segments analyzed (5 buildups, 5 orgasms)
- Total analyzed duration: 430 seconds (~7.2 minutes)
- Sample rate: 48,000 Hz (high quality)
- Mono conversion applied (averaged stereo channels)
